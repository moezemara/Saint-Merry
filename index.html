What is Saint Merry

Saint Merry is a group of three aspiring high school students who employ poltical and deplomatic tools available for high school and university students to raise awareness about politics and sustained development goals in Egypt.

Our
Story

Founded in 2018, the group was built off of the aspiration to create a change in the Egyptian society and support its growth. We grew to bring about a better Egypt. With our interests in politics and international affairs, the group began immediately to formulate a plan that exploits diplomatic tools in order to both gain and share experience with regard to the Sustained Development Goals (SDGs). These tools included all and everything from the United Nations, the Model United Nations, United Nations subsidiaries simulation, international peace programs, and many more. Armed with this goal in mind, Saint Merry grew off of the brain child of three high school students from STEM Egypt.
